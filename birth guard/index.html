about us

Birth Guard plan insurance is designed for all women, whether married or single, even teenagers are included into the plan, and they should be 18years and above. Whenever any woman signed to this plan gets pregnant or they are on their childbirth, birth guard comes in for a full support, with the baby’s layette for instance, baby stroller, soft hooded baby towels, formula milk, bottles, newborn teats, crib or bassinet and many more.

It offers a comprehensive maternity coverage, prenatal, childbirth, postnatal, and newborn care, affordable premiums, access to network hospitals and healthcare providers. In our services, there is a 30% cash back for all the years payed if one does not have children in five years of being insured.

Another key aspect of Birth Guard insurance is its commitment to providing personalized support and assistance throughout the maternity journey. This includes access to dedicated support team or hotline available 24 hours or 24/7 to address any questions or concerns that policyholders may have during pregnancy, childbirth, and the postnatal period.

People need to be insured because insurance helps guard one’s financial future. By paying a monthly premium, one is saved from paying out of pocket for medical expenses, the insurance policy will cover the costs, Beginner (2024). Insurance planning works to reduce the financial risks related to health issues.
